Brownies Recipe

THE BEST BROWNIE RECIPE YOU COULD ASK FOR!

Have the most gooey and softest brownies ever!

This brownie recipe provides the perfect balance of fudgy,gooey,cakey and sweet.

Enjoy the best brownies you'll ever have with just a few ingredients and steps!

INGREDIENTS:

  • Two spoons of Butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 spoon of cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • Either dark chocolate or milk chocolate(whichever you prefer)

STEPS:

  1. To a bowl add 1 egg and a cup of sugar
  2. Whisk until all the sugar melts
  3. Add two spoons of butter to a pan and melt until it turns golden brown
  4. To your melted butter add a spoon of cocoa powder and mix well
  5. Take the contents of your pan(the butter and cocoa that you mixed earlier)and add it to your bowl containing the sugar and egg
  6. Mix the contents well and then add flour and a pinch of salt
  7. Mix everything together untils it forms a fine paste
  8. Chop up your chocolates(if they are chocolate chips just add them the way they are)and add it to your brownie paste
  9. Get your baking pan and apply oil and a little bit of flour(This is to make sure the brownies dont stick later after you have baked them!)
  10. Preheat your oven at 180 degrees celcius for 3 minutes
  11. Finally insert your baking pan in the oven and wait for 10 minutes

AND THERE YOU HAVE IT,THE BEST BROWNIES YOU COULD EVER ASK FOR!
